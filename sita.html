Sita


"Don't be Sita" ,They roar...
"So yielding, so demure!!!
Betrothed on conditions met, swayam var was a hoax...
A valient act it was in all, no love coaxed!!!
And then
Abducted without your mistake, how do you justify that????
Wasn't it not outraging your modesty act?
How many times should you face the pointed fingers?
Purged in fire, the rage should have lingered!!!"
"Oh no " I bemused," don't get me wrong, I don't see it as a pedestal..
No desire to be eulogised, worshipped or being eternal...
The exile was my decision, none compell...
Bounded by a line, the kidnap was a fortell
What ever happened, it was destined to be...
Because if there was no Sita, The demon king wouldn't hv met his end,
Ramayan would have ceased to be!!!!
